About the App

UseBy helps you track expiry dates, subscriptions, EMI payments, savings goals, warranties, and insurance renewals — all in one secure and simple app.

Key Features

Everything you need to stay on top of what matters.

🔐 Secure mobile authentication
🛒 Track grocery and food expiry dates
🛡️ Monitor warranty and insurance renewals
📺 Track subscriptions and renewal reminders
💳 Manage EMI due dates
💰 Track savings goals
🔔 Local notifications for reminders
⏱️ Optional app lock (On Close / 24 Hours)
☀️ Stay unlocked 24h after authentication
🌓 Light and Dark theme support
📴 Works offline for core tracking
📷 Camera support for quick entry
