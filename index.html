About the App

UseBy helps you track expiry dates for groceries, medicines, warranties, and insurance policies — all in one simple offline app.

Key Features

  • Track grocery and food expiry dates
  • Monitor warranty and insurance renewals
  • Works completely offline
  • Simple and clean interface
  • Camera support for quick entry
  • No login required
